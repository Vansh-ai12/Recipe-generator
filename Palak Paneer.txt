Title: Palak Paneer Recipe
Palak Paneer is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a smooth, spiced spinach gravy. It's healthy, flavorful, and pairs perfectly with roti or rice.

Ingredients:
Paneer - 200g, cubed
Spinach (Palak) - 3-4 cups, washed and blanched
Onion - 1 medium, chopped
Tomato - 1 medium, chopped
Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
Green chilies- 1-2, chopped (optional)
Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
Red chili powder - 1 tsp
Garam masala - 1/2 tsp
Cream - 2 tbsp (optional, for richness)
Salt - to taste
Oil or ghee - 2 tbsp


Instructions:
Blanch the Spinach: Boil water in a pot, add spinach leaves, and cook for 2-3 minutes. Drain and immediately rinse with cold water. Blend into a smooth puree.

Cook the Gravy: Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds, then saut√© onions, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies until golden.

Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
Add the spinach puree, cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat, and adjust salt.
Add Paneer: Gently add paneer cubes to the spinach gravy. Cook for 3-4 minutes, allowing the paneer to soak in the flavors.

Finish: Stir in garam masala and cream (optional). Simmer for 2 minutes.

Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with roti or rice.
