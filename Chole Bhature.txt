Title: Chole Bhature Recipe


Chole Bhature is a delicious North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas (chole) and fluffy fried bread (bhature). Perfect for a hearty breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients:

For Chole:
- 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
- 2 large onions (finely chopped)
- 2 tomatoes (pureed)
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 2-3 green chilies (slit)
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 2 bay leaves
- 1-inch cinnamon stick
- 2-3 cloves
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp chole masala (optional)
- Salt to taste
- 3 tbsp oil
- Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

For Bhature:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
- 2 tbsp semolina (sooji/rava)
- 1/2 cup yogurt (dahi)
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- Water (for kneading)
- Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions:

For Chole:
1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt and water until soft (3-4 whistles).
2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cinnamon, and cloves.
3. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
4. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for a minute.
5. Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
6. Add cooked chickpeas along with water and simmer for 10 minutes.
7. Sprinkle garam masala and chole masala. Adjust seasoning and garnish with coriander leaves.

For Bhature:
1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, semolina, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt.
2. Add yogurt and knead into a soft dough using water as needed.
3. Cover and let the dough rest for 2-3 hours.
4. Divide the dough into equal portions, roll into balls, and flatten into discs.
5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry bhature until golden and puffed.

Serve hot with chole, pickles, and onions!

Enjoy your delicious Chole Bhature!
